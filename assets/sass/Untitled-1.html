Corporate Social Responsibility

At Salim & Brothers, we firmly believe in running our business responsibly and sustainably so that we can bring security and wellbeing to our stakeholders, community, and society, as a whole.

We Acknowledge, we are accountable to the earth & all of its living creatures, beyond our products and services. We are responsible for our own actions & our actions should contribute to the betterment of society & the environment.

We also spread our helping hand to those non-profit organizations that focus their skills & energy on important environmental challenges.

Quality Policy

We strive to achieve excellence by ensuring that every moment of our time is spent in adding value, making sure that we do things right, the first time, and every time.

With this quest for quality, Salim & Brothers have always taken initiatives in bringing our services into a new arena; from ledger to office automation; from general solution to tailor-made solution; delivery time from days to hours.

We continuously maintain, develop as well as improve our products and services quality management system in accordance with the necessary requirements.

At Salim & Brothers, we adhere to and contribute to the effectiveness of our quality system in every aspect of our business. Our key focus is complete client satisfaction, for that we provide our clients with uncompromising quality products & services. Our Quality Assurance system is guided by the principles that we believe and support in our working culture.

Environmental Policy

Our Environmental policy is driven by our commitment to preserve and protect the environment, the way we do our business.

We are committed to proactively address all the climate change issues with the efficient use of natural resources & energy. We always follow the Green Environment Policy. Our Purpose is and always should be to promote a greener and healthier environment.
